The Stasis Device sells for **15.6 million credits** and is the most profitable single craft in No Man's Sky. One device, crafted from scratch, is worth more than most players make in hours of grinding. If you're serious about building wealth, this is the craft to learn.

The catch: it takes three major sub-components, each of which requires its own chain of materials. This guide breaks down every step — what you need, where to get it, and how to build toward a farm that makes Stasis Devices on repeat.

---

## What You Need to Craft a Stasis Device

The top-level recipe requires three items:

| Component | Quantity |
|---|---|
| Quantum Processor | 1 |
| Cryogenic Chamber | 1 |
| Iridesite | 1 |

None of these are craftable without first building their own sub-components. Here's the full tree.

---

## Full Crafting Tree

### Quantum Processor
**Crafts from:** 1× Circuit Board + 1× Superconductor

**Circuit Board** crafts from:
- 1× Heat Capacitor → 100× Frost Crystal + 200× Solanium
- 1× Poly Fibre → 100× Cactus Flesh + 200× Star Bulb

**Superconductor** crafts from:
- 1× Semiconductor → 1× Thermic Condensate + 1× Nitrogen Salt
- 1× Enriched Carbon → 250× Radon + 50× Condensed Carbon

**Thermic Condensate** crafts from: 250× Sulphurine + 50× Condensed Carbon  
**Nitrogen Salt** crafts from: 250× Nitrogen + 50× Condensed Carbon

## Step-by-Step Crafting Order

Work bottom-up — craft the base components first, then combine upward.

### Step 1: Craft the Alloys (for Iridesite)

1. Craft **Aronium**: 50× Paraffinium + 50× Ionised Cobalt
2. Craft **Magno-gold**: 50× Phosphorus + 50× Ionised Cobalt
3. Craft **Grantine**: 50× Dioxite + 50× Ionised Cobalt
4. Combine all three → **Iridesite**

### Step 2: Craft the Intermediates (for Cryogenic Chamber)

1. Run your Condensed Carbon through the refiner if you're short — Carbon refines 1:1 to Condensed Carbon
2. Craft **Thermic Condensate**: 250× Sulphurine + 50× Condensed Carbon
3. Craft **Nitrogen Salt**: 250× Nitrogen + 50× Condensed Carbon
4. Craft **Enriched Carbon**: 250× Radon + 50× Condensed Carbon
5. Craft **HoT Ice**: 1× Enriched Carbon + 1× Nitrogen Salt
6. Craft **Glass** (×5): 40× Frost Crystal each
7. Craft **Lubricant**: 50× Faecium + 400× Gamma Root
8. Craft **Living Glass**: 1× Lubricant + 5× Glass
9. Combine HoT Ice + Thermic Condensate → **Cryo-pump**
10. Combine Living Glass + Cryo-pump → **Cryogenic Chamber**

### Step 3: Craft the Intermediates (for Quantum Processor)

1. Craft **Heat Capacitor**: 100× Frost Crystal + 200× Solanium
2. Craft **Poly Fibre**: 100× Cactus Flesh + 200× Star Bulb
3. Craft **Circuit Board**: 1× Heat Capacitor + 1× Poly Fibre
4. Craft **Thermic Condensate**: 250× Sulphurine + 50× Condensed Carbon
5. Craft **Nitrogen Salt**: 250× Nitrogen + 50× Condensed Carbon
6. Craft **Semiconductor**: 1× Thermic Condensate + 1× Nitrogen Salt
7. Craft **Enriched Carbon**: 250× Radon + 50× Condensed Carbon
8. Combine Semiconductor + Enriched Carbon → **Superconductor**
9. Combine Circuit Board + Superconductor → **Quantum Processor**

### Step 4: Final Assembly

Combine Quantum Processor + Cryogenic Chamber + Iridesite → **Stasis Device** ✓

Sell at the Galactic Trade Terminal for **15,600,000 credits**.

## How to Farm This Efficiently

Doing this once is a grind. Doing it on repeat requires a proper farm.

**You need two types of farms:**

**Crop farms** — for the plant-based materials. Set up planters for:
- Frost Crystal (frozen planet biome plants)
- Solanium (scorched biome)
- Cactus Flesh (arid biome)
- Star Bulb (lush biome)
- Faecium (toxic biome)
- Gamma Root (irradiated biome)

Six crop types is a lot. Most players build one large base with biome-specific indoor planters using supplied terrain manipulation, or use Agricultural Terminals to hire farmers who boost yield.

**Atmospheric Harvesters** — for the gases. You need massive quantities of Sulphurine, Radon, and Nitrogen. Build multiple harvesters on appropriate planets and let them run between play sessions. In my experience, 4-6 harvesters per gas planet is enough to keep up with regular crafting.

The Condensed Carbon, Ionised Cobalt, and planetary minerals (Paraffinium, Phosphorus, Dioxite) are small enough quantities that you can mine them during normal gameplay without a dedicated farm.

## Is the Stasis Device Worth Crafting?

Yes — it's the best credit-per-slot item you can make. A single Stasis Device in one inventory slot is worth 15.6M credits. For comparison, most raw materials sell for under 1,000 credits per unit.

The full farm setup takes time to build, but once it's running, you can craft one or two Stasis Devices per play session without leaving your base. That's 30M+ credits in a few hours of collection and crafting.

If you're not ready to build the full farm yet, you can still craft one-off devices by buying the gases and plants from the Galactic Trade Network — though that cuts into your margin significantly.

## Blueprints

All intermediate blueprints (Circuit Board, Superconductor, etc.) are available from the Synthesis Laboratory on the Space Anomaly for Nanites, or found in Manufacturing Facilities. The Stasis Device blueprint itself is unlocked through the crafting milestone chain once you've crafted enough advanced products.
