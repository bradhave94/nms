The Fusion Igniter sells for **15.6 million credits** and is tied with the [Stasis Device](/blog/how-to-craft-stasis-device) as the most valuable item you can craft in No Man's Sky. Same sell price, different crafting path — and depending on which materials you have easier access to, one might be a better fit for your setup than the other.

This guide covers the complete crafting tree, every raw material you need, and how to put together a farm that makes Fusion Igniters on repeat.

---

## What You Need to Craft a Fusion Igniter

The top-level recipe requires three items:

| Component | Quantity |
|---|---|
| Portable Reactor | 1 |
| Quantum Processor | 1 |
| Geodesite | 1 |

Each requires its own chain of sub-components. Here's the full breakdown.

---

## Full Crafting Tree

### Portable Reactor
**Crafts from:** 1× Liquid Explosive + 1× Fusion Accelerant

**Liquid Explosive** crafts from:
- 1× Acid → 25× Mordite + 600× Fungal Mould
- 1× Unstable Gel → 200× Cactus Flesh

**Fusion Accelerant** crafts from:
- 1× Organic Catalyst → 1× Thermic Condensate + 1× Enriched Carbon
- 1× Nitrogen Salt → 250× Nitrogen + 50× Condensed Carbon

**Thermic Condensate** crafts from: 250× Sulphurine + 50× Condensed Carbon  
**Enriched Carbon** crafts from: 250× Radon + 50× Condensed Carbon

---

### Quantum Processor
**Crafts from:** 1× Circuit Board + 1× Superconductor

**Circuit Board** crafts from:
- 1× Heat Capacitor → 100× Frost Crystal + 200× Solanium
- 1× Poly Fibre → 100× Cactus Flesh + 200× Star Bulb

**Superconductor** crafts from:
- 1× Semiconductor → 1× Thermic Condensate + 1× Nitrogen Salt
- 1× Enriched Carbon → 250× Radon + 50× Condensed Carbon

**Thermic Condensate** crafts from: 250× Sulphurine + 50× Condensed Carbon  
**Nitrogen Salt** crafts from: 250× Nitrogen + 50× Condensed Carbon

---

### Geodesite
**Crafts from:** 1× Dirty Bronze + 1× Herox + 1× Lemmium

| Alloy | Recipe |
|---|---|
| Dirty Bronze | 50× Pyrite + 100× Pure Ferrite |
| Herox | 50× Ammonia + 50× Ionised Cobalt |
| Lemmium | 50× Uranium + 100× Pure Ferrite |

## Complete Raw Materials List

Here's every base resource you need to craft one Fusion Igniter from scratch:

| Resource | Amount Needed | Source |
|---|---|---|
| Fungal Mould | 600 | Farm (Fungal Mould plant, toxic planets) |
| Cactus Flesh | 300 | Farm (Cactus plant, arid/desert planets) |
| Frost Crystal | 100 | Farm (Frost Crystal plant, frozen planets) |
| Solanium | 200 | Farm (Solanium plant, hot/scorched planets) |
| Star Bulb | 200 | Farm (Star Bulb plant, lush planets) |
| Mordite | 25 | Harvest from creatures (any planet) |
| Sulphurine | 500 | Atmospheric Harvester (volcanic/hot planets) |
| Radon | 500 | Atmospheric Harvester (stormy planets) |
| Nitrogen | 500 | Atmospheric Harvester (lush planets) |
| Condensed Carbon | 300 | Mine (carbon crystals) or refine Carbon |
| Pyrite | 50 | Mine (arid/desert planets) |
| Pure Ferrite | 200 | Refine Ferrite Dust (1:1 ratio) |
| Ammonia | 50 | Mine (toxic planets) |

The Fungal Mould requirement (600 units) is the standout here — it's the biggest farm crop you'll need and the one most players don't have set up by default. Without a farm, you're looking at a lot of time on toxic planets.

## How the Fusion Igniter Compares to the Stasis Device

Both sell for 15.6M credits. The choice between them comes down to your farm setup:

| | Fusion Igniter | Stasis Device |
|---|---|---|
| Sell value | 15,600,000 cr | 15,600,000 cr |
| Shared component | Quantum Processor | Quantum Processor |
| Unique crops needed | Fungal Mould, Cactus Flesh | Faecium, Gamma Root, Star Bulb, Frost Crystal |
| Unique minerals | Pyrite, Pure Ferrite, Ammonia, Uranium | Paraffinium, Phosphorus, Dioxite |
| Ionised Cobalt | 50 | 150 |
| Gas requirements | Same (500 each) | Same (500 each) |

If you already farm Faecium and Gamma Root for other recipes, the Stasis Device might be easier to slot into your existing setup. If you're on a toxic planet base and have Fungal Mould in abundance, the Fusion Igniter makes more sense. Some players farm both alternately to maximise credit output per session.

## Step-by-Step Crafting Order

### Step 1: Craft the Alloys (for Geodesite)

1. Craft **Dirty Bronze**: 50× Pyrite + 100× Pure Ferrite
2. Craft **Herox**: 50× Ammonia + 50× Ionised Cobalt
3. Craft **Lemmium**: 50× Uranium + 100× Pure Ferrite
4. Combine all three → **Geodesite**

### Step 2: Craft the Intermediates (for Portable Reactor)

1. Craft **Thermic Condensate**: 250× Sulphurine + 50× Condensed Carbon
2. Craft **Enriched Carbon**: 250× Radon + 50× Condensed Carbon
3. Craft **Nitrogen Salt**: 250× Nitrogen + 50× Condensed Carbon
4. Craft **Organic Catalyst**: 1× Thermic Condensate + 1× Enriched Carbon
5. Craft **Fusion Accelerant**: 1× Organic Catalyst + 1× Nitrogen Salt
6. Craft **Acid**: 25× Mordite + 600× Fungal Mould
7. Craft **Unstable Gel**: 200× Cactus Flesh
8. Craft **Liquid Explosive**: 1× Acid + 1× Unstable Gel
9. Combine Liquid Explosive + Fusion Accelerant → **Portable Reactor**

### Step 3: Craft the Intermediates (for Quantum Processor)

1. Craft **Heat Capacitor**: 100× Frost Crystal + 200× Solanium
2. Craft **Poly Fibre**: 100× Cactus Flesh + 200× Star Bulb
3. Craft **Circuit Board**: 1× Heat Capacitor + 1× Poly Fibre
4. Craft **Thermic Condensate**: 250× Sulphurine + 50× Condensed Carbon
5. Craft **Nitrogen Salt**: 250× Nitrogen + 50× Condensed Carbon
6. Craft **Semiconductor**: 1× Thermic Condensate + 1× Nitrogen Salt
7. Craft **Enriched Carbon**: 250× Radon + 50× Condensed Carbon
8. Combine Semiconductor + Enriched Carbon → **Superconductor**
9. Combine Circuit Board + Superconductor → **Quantum Processor**

### Step 4: Final Assembly

Combine Portable Reactor + Quantum Processor + Geodesite → **Fusion Igniter** ✓

Sell at the Galactic Trade Terminal for **15,600,000 credits**.

## Farming Tips

**Fungal Mould is the bottleneck.** You need 600 per craft, which is a lot if you're collecting it manually from toxic planets. Set up a dedicated Fungal Mould farm — it grows fast in indoor planters and a single medium-sized farm can produce several hundred units per hour.

**Mordite is easy.** You only need 25, which you can collect in a few minutes by harvesting creatures on any planet. It's not worth building a dedicated farm for such a small quantity.

**The three gases are the same as the Stasis Device** — 500 each of Sulphurine, Radon, and Nitrogen. If you're already running Atmospheric Harvesters for one item, you're set for both. Build harvesters on 4-6 appropriate planets per gas type to keep supply consistent.

**Pure Ferrite is easy to source** — just refine Ferrite Dust at a 1:1 ratio. Mine Ferrite Dust anywhere and run it through a Medium Refiner.
